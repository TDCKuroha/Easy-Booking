What do I have to do to request an invoice?

Go to the confirmation email or confirmation page and click "Request an invoice." Complete the form and send your request. If you don't see the link, go to the Help page.

I have been charged the wrong way. What should I do?

If EasyBooking.com has charged you by mistake, go to the Help page. We may ask you for a copy of your bank statement showing the incorrect charges, as well as your reservation confirmation number and PIN code.

Where can I see the cancellation conditions?

EasyBooking.com will charge the total amount of the reservation on your card at the time you book. If you want more information about the payment, check the confirmation email.

Can I make changes to my reservation?

No, you cannot make changes to your reservation, such as modifying the dates of the stay, the name of the client or the e-mail.

I have paid for the reservation, but I have not received a confirmation email. What should I do?

You will receive a confirmation email as soon as the reservation is confirmed. It usually arrives within a couple of minutes, but sometimes it takes longer. Check your spam or junk folder, as the email may end up there by mistake. If you do not receive an email, please contact our Customer Service team.

Does my reservation include meals?

In the confirmation email you will see if you have any food included. Depending on the accommodation, you may be able to add them upon arrival. Contact the accommodation directly 1 or 2 days before your arrival for more information.
